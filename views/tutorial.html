Welcome to Octopus Crapshoot

Octopus Crapshoot is an engaging dice-based gambling game that offers thrilling opportunities to test your luck and strategy.

Prepare to embark on an exhilarating adventure as you roll a pair of dice and aim to achieve the optimal outcome.

If your initial roll results in a total of 7 or 11, your luck runs dry, and you face the unfortunate consequence of losing the round.

However,

If your roll doesn't yield one of those unfavorable totals, rejoice! You earn a valuable point. Each successful roll adds to your tally, propelling you towards greater heights of achievement. The ultimate goal is to accumulate the highest score possible without succumbing to the risk of busting .

Should you desire to intensify the thrill and potentially reap greater rewards, you have the option to double down. By doing so, the points you gain match the total of your roll! However, be aware that this decision also amplifies the perils. Doubling down exposes you to the risk of busting not only with a 7 or 11, but also with a 2 or a 12.
