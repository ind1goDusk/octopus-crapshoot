How to Play:

Octopus Crapshoot is a simple, dice-based gambling game.

To begin, roll a pair of dice and add the two numbers together.

If you rolled a total of a 7 or 11, sorry, you lose!

BUT

If you didn't roll one of those two totals, you gain a point.Each time you roll the dice without losing, you gain more points. The object of the game is to score as high as you possibly can without busting.

If you want to risk more for a higher reward, you can double down. When you do, you gain points equal to whatever total you roll!
BUT
with higher points comes more danger. If you double down, you will bust if you roll a 2 or a 12 as well as a 7 or 11.
